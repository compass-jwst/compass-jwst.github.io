Timeline

Our collaboration is actively working on the analysis of all planets awarded through JWST GO #2512. The data available through our program is completely open and available on MAST immediately after the data is taken. Please see the COMPASS Team Statement on "Scooping" and the Value of Open Data. Below you can see which of our data have arrived, and which of our team members are leading the analysis.

We kindly ask that the community send all questions and inquiries to the co-PIs Natasha Batalha & Johanna Teske. For futher details and questions regarding how to get involved please see our Collaboration Guidelines.
